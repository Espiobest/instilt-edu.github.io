Our Mission

Our mission is to level the playing field between the children that come from more fortunate backgrounds and those who don't possess the same privilege.

Our Model

In an effort to grow and deepen the understanding of our students, we use a unique model. For our faculty, we pair up one native English speaker with another tutor proficient in the language of the students. This way students are able to enjoy the best of both worlds as they are pushed to communicate seamlessly with a qualified tutor who speaks English in their day-to-day allowing them some great outside exposure. At the same time, the other tutor is always to ensure that the students are following, maintaining a comfortable and familiar environment for the children.

Our Method
The classes are primarily held on Zoom, and the children are free to unmute themselves and ask questions at any point outside of the continuous interaction that our tutors maintain. All other communication, including the distribution of Homework assignments, is all done through a Whatsapp group. Here, too, students are free to clarify any questions they may have.
Our classes usually start off with an engaging activity to get the children going, these activities are usually directly related to the English content. For example, we play crossword to strengthen the vocabulary of the children. After which we would usually discuss the homework that the students were previously assigned and allow them time to clear out any doubts that may have come up.
Then we move on to the subject matter, which is part of a comprehensive curriculum curated by our team. The curriculum has an array of English grammar lessons inspired from reputable Indian boards like CBSE, the American Schooling system as well as International sources like Khan academy.
We understand the pressure that students in these situations undergo, hence we allocate minimal homework and try to get through an adequate amount of practice exercises in class itself. We are also incredibly flexible in our approach and strive to meet the needs of the students, always.
